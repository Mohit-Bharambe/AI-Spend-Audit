# Landing Page Copy Strategy

This document outlines the core messaging and copywriting frameworks used for SpendLens to maximize conversion from site visit to audit completion.

## Core Value Proposition

**Headline:** Stop Overpaying For AI Tools.
**Sub-headline:** Startups are wasting thousands on unused seats, redundant tools, and overlapping AI tiers. Run a free audit in 60 seconds and find out exactly how much budget you can recover today.

## Primary Call to Action (CTA)

**Button Copy:** Run My Free Audit
**Microcopy (beneath button):** No credit card required. No login needed. Results in 60s.

## The Three Pillars of Value
1. **Identify Seat Waste:** Stop paying for Enterprise minimums you aren't using. We find the fat in your subscription layer.
2. **Consolidate Your Stack:** You likely don't need Cursor Business, ChatGPT Team, *and* Claude Pro. We tell you what to cut and what to keep.
3. **Optimize API Spend:** Shift raw OpenAI usage to wholesale credits and instantly cut infrastructure costs by 20%.

## Why this Copy Works
The headline is direct, agitated, and immediately clear. By telling the user to "Stop Overpaying," we invoke loss aversion. The microcopy explicitly removes friction ("No login needed") to drive them straight into the deterministic audit engine without hesitation.
